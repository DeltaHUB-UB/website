## Extended Deadline: Postdoctoral Researcher Positions at DELTA-HUB

We are pleased to announce that the application deadline for our **two Postdoctoral Researcher positions** has been extended to **15 March 2026**.

If you missed the original deadline or are still preparing your application, this is your chance to join the DELTA-HUB team at the University of Bucharest and conduct high-impact research in the Danube Delta.

### The Positions

We are looking for motivated researchers for two distinct profiles:

**Position 1: SWOT Data & Global Delta Analysis**

Focus on developing new data products from SWOT and NISAR satellite missions, conducting global-scale analysis of delta dynamics, and studying water connectivity and sediment interactions.

**Position 2: Field Data Analysis & Model Calibration**

Bridge observations with numerical modelling by processing data from hydrodynamic and sediment sensors, developing calibration relationships, and validating models (Delft3D, MIKE21).

### What We Offer

- **Contract**: 2-year contract starting Spring-Summer 2026
- **Salary**: Competitive remuneration of €4,000/month (gross)
- **Environment**: A hands-on research environment combining satellite remote sensing, in-situ measurements, and numerical modelling, with direct access to the Danube Delta
- **Support**: Funding for fieldwork campaigns, international conference travel, and training opportunities

### How to Apply

To apply, please submit your CV, a brief motivation letter, and two referee letters to **delta.hub@geo.unibuc.ro**.

**New Application Deadline: 15 March 2026**
