## Delta Dunării. O istorie în imagini

As part of **Filmul Științific Românesc** at **Cinemateca Eforie**, we invite you to a screening of **"Delta Dunării. O istorie în imagini"** — a selection of Romanian documentaries about the Danube Delta filmed between **1911 and 1975**.

**When:** Wednesday, 3 June 2026, 20:00 · and Sunday, 7 June 2026, 20:15

**Where:** Cinemateca Eforie

Each screening is followed by a **Q&A** with **Ana Szel**, **Florin Zăinescu**, and **Ovidiu Daneș**.

The programme traces how the Danube Delta has been seen and recorded on film across the 20th century, bringing together rare archival footage that documents the landscape, communities, and transformations of the Delta.
