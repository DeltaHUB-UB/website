## DELTA-Hub Lecture — River Deltas at the Crossroads

We are pleased to invite you to a **DELTA-Hub Lecture**:

### River Deltas at the Crossroads: State of the Art, Emerging Threats, and the Role of DELTA-Hub

**Speaker:** Prof. Edward Anthony, ERA Chair Holder (CEREGE, Aix-Marseille University)

**When:** Tuesday, 2 June 2026, 16:00

**Where:** Faculty of Geography, Amphitheatre George Vâlsan (3rd floor), University of Bucharest

River deltas are among the most valuable and most vulnerable landscapes on Earth. This lecture reviews the current state of the art in delta science, examines the emerging threats deltas face — from sediment starvation and subsidence to sea-level rise and human pressure — and presents the role of DELTA-Hub in advancing research and management of the Danube Delta and deltas worldwide.

The lecture is open to students, researchers, and everyone interested in delta science.

More information: [https://delta-hub.unibuc.ro/](https://delta-hub.unibuc.ro/)
