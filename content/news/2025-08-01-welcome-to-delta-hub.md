## Welcome to DELTA-HUB

We are thrilled to announce the official launch of the DELTA-HUB project website! This platform will serve as your primary source for updates, research findings, and educational resources related to our mission of understanding and protecting the Danube Delta.

### What to Expect

Over the coming months, we will be sharing:

- **Research Updates**: Latest findings from our digital twin modeling efforts
- **Educational Programs**: Information about our Master's program and training opportunities
- **Team News**: Meet our growing team of delta experts
- **Workshop Announcements**: Upcoming events and collaboration opportunities

### Get Involved

Whether you're a researcher, student, policymaker, or simply interested in delta ecosystems, we invite you to follow our journey. Check back regularly for updates, or connect with us on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/delta-hub-river-delta-science-education-and-modeling-hub/).

Together, we can ensure a sustainable future for the Danube Delta and deltas worldwide.
