About Delta-Hub

Project Goals

The Delta-Hub Horizon Europe project aims to create a comprehensive framework for sustainable delta management across Europe, addressing the complex challenges posed by climate change, urbanization, and environmental degradation.

Our primary objectives include developing innovative monitoring technologies, fostering international collaboration, and creating actionable policy recommendations for delta regions throughout Europe.

Methodology

Our interdisciplinary approach combines cutting-edge remote sensing technologies, advanced data analytics, and participatory research methods. We employ a multi-scale analysis framework that integrates local community knowledge with scientific expertise.

Remote Sensing

Advanced satellite monitoring and drone-based data collection for comprehensive environmental assessment.

Participatory Research

Engaging local communities and stakeholders in the research process to ensure practical and applicable solutions.

Expected Outcomes

Delta-Hub will deliver a suite of practical tools and recommendations that will significantly impact how European river deltas are managed and protected.

Policy Recommendations

Evidence-based guidelines for sustainable delta management policies

Management Tools

Practical instruments for delta monitoring and assessment

Capacity Building

Training programs and knowledge transfer initiatives
